Arrive at or In

Arrive At on in?


             Arrive 
           /        \
         /            \
     WHEN               WHERE
   /  |   \             /     \
at    on    in         at       in
5pm   Sun   July      airport   London

WHEN

  • at: most specific period of time: hour, minute.
  • on: wider then a specific time: one date, one day
  • in : year, month

WHERE

  • at: specific place. doesn't matter if the name is specified or not. eg: airport, hotel
  • in: city, country

Exceptions

No prepositions for yesterday, last year, next week, home
